not a manifesto

When was the last time you witnessed something so quintessentially crypto？


Notfellows is part of a generational movement, unbound by norms. Young and scrappy builders thousands of miles apart have found each other in an evergreen cloud city. After missing our shot with a prestigious fellowship, we transformed the lost opportunity into a new one.

Thanks to this serendipitous encounter, in an age of individualism and alienation, we have found solace in the community, strength in friendships, and hope in a shared vision of what we can help the world become.


14 years ago, Satoshi invented Bitcoin, an era-defining technology and a sociopolitical revolution that would forever change the world and all of our lives.

14 years later, standing on the shoulders of giants, we are humbled to be building in this industry and grateful for those taking a chance on us, the young and less seasoned talent. We want to invite you along our journey of growth for the decades to come.

This is just the beginning, and we can’t wait to share with you what the future holds.
